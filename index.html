About Me

I am Gabriel Uwaila, born on December 9, 2003, in Antwerp and currently residing in Sint-Niklaas. My passion for technology traces back to my early years, driven by a resourceful mindset cultivated out of necessity. Growing up, I wasn't always fortunate enough to have access to the latest games or machines. However, this limitation sparked my ingenuity, leading me to find alternative ways to indulge in my interests. 🕹️ From a young age, I immersed myself in the world of technology, leveraging my Android phone to emulate old Pokémon games and exploring virtual machines on the family PC to experiment with different operating systems. This early exposure instilled in me a proactive mentality, always seeking innovative solutions to pursue my passions.

My academic journey reflects this innate curiosity and problem-solving approach. Currently studying Computer Science at Karel de Grote University College, with classes conducted entirely in English, I continue to explore the endless possibilities within the realm of technology. Beyond academia, I have a profound love for music 🎶, which has been a constant source of joy and self-expression for me. I started playing the drums as a toddler and have since expanded my musical repertoire to include the piano and bass guitar.

In addition to my passion for technology and music, I have a deep interest in fashion and modeling. Over the years, I have had the opportunity to collaborate with local brands, participating in photo shoots and campaigns. The response on social media, particularly on platforms like TikTok, has been overwhelming, with my creativity and expression resonating with audiences. 👕📸

Furthermore, sports have played a significant role in shaping my character. Despite experiencing setbacks such as an ACL tear that halted my athletic career, my dedication to physical fitness remains unwavering. I commit to fitness routines four times a week to stay in shape, and I still relish the opportunity to engage in friendly games of football or basketball with friends. ⚽🏀

This blend of technological curiosity, creative expression through music and fashion, resilience in the face of challenges, and dedication to physical fitness encapsulates the multifaceted nature of my personality and aspirations.
